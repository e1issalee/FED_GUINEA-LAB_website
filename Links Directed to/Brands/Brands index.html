Welcome to GUINEA+LAB! We are a skincare brand to create a revolutionary experience that fuses effective skincare solutions with a touch of playful entertainment. Our project aims to redefine beauty routines, offering a curated collection of skincare essentials from renowned brands. The website revolves around a membership program where users earn "face masks" with every purchase – tokens that unlock a world of skincare adventures and exclusive perks. What sets us apart is our commitment to affordability without compromising quality. By collaborating with other esteemed skincare brands, we bring you their renowned products at a more accessible price point, ensuring that luxury skincare is within everyone's reach.

ASIA

Anua

Anua is a Korean beauty brand that embraces a holistic approach to beauty. With a foundation rooted in the heart of South Korea, Anua has set its sights on a singular mission: to harness the power of natural, non-harmful ingredients in the pursuit of naturally beautiful and healthy skin. Dedicated to delivering skincare solutions that cater to all skin types, Anua takes a special interest in nurturing sensitive and trouble-prone skin.

Glow Recipe

They create gentle yet effective formulas to deliver clinically proven results like no other. They formulate with the highest quality ingredients including antioxidant-rich fruit extracts and clinically proven actives, but what's not in their formulas is just as important as what is. They provide full ingredient lists for complete transparency, and their products are always clean, vegan, and Leaping Bunny certified cruelty-free.

Laneige

Laneige is a renowned South Korean skincare and cosmetics brand, celebrated for its focus on hydration and skincare innovation. As part of Amorepacific Corporation, one of South Korea's major beauty companies, Laneige is globally recognized for its Water Sleeping Mask, a popular product designed to provide intensive overnight hydration. The brand incorporates advanced technologies like Water Science™ into its formulations and offers a diverse range of skincare and cosmetics products. Embracing the K-Beauty trend, Laneige has gained international popularity and maintains a commitment to delivering effective, clean, and cruelty-free products.

NORTH AMERICA

The Ordinary

The Ordinary is a renowned skincare brand recognized for its simplicity, transparency, and affordability. Under the DECIEM umbrella, the brand prioritizes effective formulations with proven active ingredients, providing detailed concentration information. Known for straightforward and affordable products, The Ordinary adheres to cruelty-free practices, appealing to consumers seeking accessible and ethical skincare solutions.

EUROPE

La Roche-Posay

La Roche-Posay is a French skincare brand renowned for dermatologist-tested products designed for sensitive skin. Utilizing thermal spring water from La Roche-Posay, known for its soothing properties, the brand offers effective solutions for various skincare concerns, including acne and aging. A part of the L'Oréal Group, La Roche-Posay prioritizes efficacy, tolerance, and safety, making it a trusted choice for those with sensitive or problematic skin.
